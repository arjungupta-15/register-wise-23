## üöÄ Next Steps to Get on Google

### Step 1: Google Search Console Setup

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click "Add Property"
3. Enter: `https://tarseducation.in`
4. Verify ownership (choose HTML file method):
   - Download verification file
   - Upload to `public/` folder
   - Click verify

### Step 2: Submit Sitemap

1. In Google Search Console
2. Go to "Sitemaps" (left sidebar)
3. Enter: `https://tarseducation.in/sitemap.xml`
4. Click "Submit"

### Step 3: Google My Business (Optional but Recommended)

1. Go to: https://business.google.com
2. Create business profile
3. Add:
   - Business name: "TARS Education"
   - Category: "Computer Training School"
   - Address (if physical location)
   - Phone number
   - Website: https://tarseducation.in
   - Photos of institute/logo

### Step 4: Build Backlinks
